PRIVACY & SECURITY

FlashDrop Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. All transfers use direct peer-to-peer connections with end-to-end encryption. Files and messages are transmitted directly between connected users and never pass through, are uploaded to, or stored on FlashDrop Pro servers.

Once a session ends or the browser page is closed, all temporary data is immediately destroyed. FlashDrop Pro does not retain backups, logs, or historical records of user activity.

COMPLIANCE & TRUST
