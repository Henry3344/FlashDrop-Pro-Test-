PRIVACY & SECURITY

All transfers are designed to occur directly between connected peers using encrypted connections. FlashDrop Pro does not upload, store, or retain files, messages, or session data. Closing the page immediately ends the session and destroys temporary data.

COMPLIANCE & TRUST

No registration, no user profiles, and no behavioral tracking. FlashDrop Pro does not collect or monetize personal data. Designed around privacy-first and data minimization principles commonly aligned with GDPR-style requirements.
